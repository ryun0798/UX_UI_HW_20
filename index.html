WellB

The objective of this project is to build a mobile app that helps people plan their next trip during post-Pandemic. After the pandemic hit, we can all say that our life has drastically changed. From working from home to attending online lectures, we were forced to adapt into this new lifestyle. Traveling has also been a challenge for many, and it requires a more cautious approach than before. With hopes of making it easier for people to travel, this project became my first project.

ROLE DURATION TOOLS
Individual 4 weeks Figma, Invision, Miro

Research Objectives

Objective 1: Discover the different thoughts users have while traveling prior to their experience.

Objective 2: Understand the different tools users use while planning a trip, and how they feel about the tools.

Objective 3: As a user researcher, I want to know the user’s thought process about traveling post-pandemic.

Problem Statement

WellB was designed to provide safe and healthy options in terms of locations and things to do for people who want to travel locally. I have observed that post pandemic traveling isn’t safe enough because there’s still a chance of getting the virus in any location, which is causing travelers to restrict themselves from traveling in general. How might we provide safe and healthy options for travelers in order to provide healthier lifestyles, help businsses, and ensure safety for people who are traveling post-pandemic?

User Persona

Based on the interviews, I came up with a generalized persona that will most likely resonate with our audience. This is Sarah Wilson. She is a 27 year old dietician who is concerned about traveling post-Pandemic. Her pain points on planning for travel and health concerns helped to give more information on building the foundation of the app goal.

User Flow

I made two user flows: One for the onboarding process, and the other one for booking a lodge.
